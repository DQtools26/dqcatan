Turn Structure

1. Roll dice → resources distributed
2. Record resources in this app
3. Optionally: build, trade, use bank, ship, rent, gamble, buy stocks
4. End turn — pass to next player

You do NOT have to build. All actions in Step 3 are optional.

Double Roll — Bank Collapse

If the same number is rolled twice in a row, the bank collapses. All deposits are lost, all loans called in immediately, and an economic downturn begins. The Bank Manager must physically perform the collapse ritual.

Winning

First player to 10 VP on their turn wins — same as standard Catan. Cannot win if: overdue loans exist, in a wait period, or during economic downturn.

Deposits

Deposited resources sit in the bank — they are locked and cannot be used directly. They are safe from the robber on a 7 roll.

Deposit Bonus: When a 7 is rolled, gain +1 resource for each different resource type you have deposited. (e.g., 3 Wood + 2 Grain deposited = +1 Wood + +1 Grain bonus)

Requires debit card or credit card to deposit.

Withdrawals

Debit Card: Withdraw directly to hand. Fee: 1 resource every 10 turns.
Full Withdrawal: Take all deposited resources at once. Cost: 1 of each resource type currently deposited. Must wait 3 turns to reopen account.
Credit Card method: Borrow against your deposits as a loan (standard CC rules apply).

Debit Card

Issued by the Banker. Allows direct deposits and debit withdrawals. Maintenance fee: 1 resource every 10 turns.

Standard Loan

• Interest: 20% of total borrowed (rounded up)
• Due: 5 turns from borrowing
• Repay principal in same resource, interest in any
• Rate increases +5% per previous late loan

If late: +25% late fee on top. Cannot buy, trade, or win. After repayment: 3-turn wait before eligible to win again.

Credit Card

Opening cost: 3 of any one resource (paid to bank)
Maintenance: 1 resource every 5 turns

Borrowing: 10% interest, due in 2 turns. Rate increases +10% per previous late CC payment.

Building discount: Ignore 1 resource cost whenever you build (road, settlement, city, dev card).

If late: +20% late fee. Cannot buy, trade, or win. 3-turn wait after repayment.

Shipping / Global Trade

Order any resource from overseas. Pay the fee in any resource except the one being ordered.

Fee scale:
• Small (1–2 units): +1 resource fee
• Medium (3–6 units): +3 resource fee
• Large (7+ units): +4 resource fee

Delivery time:
• Standard: 4 turns
• Expedited: 2 turns (fee doubled)

Suspended during economic downturns.

Rental Agreements

Any two players can create a rental agreement. The tenant "rents" a property from the landlord and pays periodically.

Rent costs:
• Settlement: 1 Wool + 1 Brick every 2 turns
• City: 1 Wood + 1 Wool + 1 Brick every 2 turns
• Road Toll: 2 Wood every 3 turns

Suspended during economic downturns.

Stock Market

Each resource type has a stock price (in resource units). Buy shares and sell later at a profit or loss.

Prices fluctuate each turn based on dice rolls. The "rich" players' dominant resources trend up; poor player resources may trend down.

Buy: Pay current price in ANY resource per share.
Sell: Receive current price in that resource per share.

Economic Events

Triggered by the Banker or by game conditions.

Economic Crisis: Rich players may be sabotaged each turn.
Economic Boom: Resource production bonuses (apply as agreed).
Recession (4 turns): No banking, shipping, rentals, or winning.
Depression (7 turns): Same as recession but longer.
