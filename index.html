🎲 Quick Rules Overview

  • Goal: Reach 10 Victory Points with no unpaid debts and no active economic restrictions.
  • Turn Steps: Roll → Distribute Resources → Optional Actions → Resolve Events → Turn Summary
  • Optional Actions: Build, Trade, Bank, Shipping, Rentals, Gamble, Stocks
  • Banking: Savings are safe, Hand resources are risky. Credit and loans enable strategic plays.
  • Events: Bank Collapse, Recession, Depression, Boom, Crisis affect player resources and stock prices.
  • Stocks: Buy low/high production dice boosts, sell high for profit.
  • Rank & Taxes: Asset-based rank determines taxation and event penalties.
  • Hand vs Assets: Hand is spendable, Assets determine wealth rank and VP.
